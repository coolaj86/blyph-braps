The Sweepstakes!

Two students will be reimbursed up to $350 dollars each for the cost of textbooks during the fall 2011 semester. This courtesy is provided by Blyph, and is subject to the following conditions:

   

Conditions and Legal Blah Blah:

Entering:

Join Blyph by entering your email address and school URL into the Blyph Books website.

Participants must be a full time student registered at a university.

Selection:

Two winners will be chosen from a random drawing of Blyph members. Blyph members can earn points that add to their chance of winning.

Sweepstakes dates:

Blyph will reimburse up to $350 of the cost of two student’s textbooks purchased within the dates of August 1st 2011 to September 30th 2011.

The random drawing will be held on October 5th and winners will be sent an email on Friday October 7th.

Distribution:

Reimbursement will be paid in the form of a check written from Blyph to the two selected students.

To claim reimbursement winners must:

Provide valid proof of purchase for each book to be reimbursed for to the Blyph team by email no later than one week after winner announcement.

If selected individual does not reply within one week, their winning status is forfeited and the drawing will be held again until a new winner is found.

Provide any other applicable reasonable info, such as full name and mailing address.

Disqualification

participants will get kicked out for:

  • Registering multiple or false accounts

  • Listing books you don’t have or need

  • Code-ninja trickery

Increase your chance of winning.

All Blyph members will receive one entry into the 2011 reimbursement drawing simply for joining.

Other things you can do to increase your chances of winning:

  • 1 additional entry will be given for each book listed on blyph.com to trade or sell.
